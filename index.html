About ProFind

ProFind is a revolutionary platform that connects customers with local professionals across various service categories. Our mission is to bridge the gap between skilled workers and those who need their services.

Our Mission

To empower local professionals by providing them with a digital platform to showcase their skills and connect with potential customers, while making it easy for users to find reliable service providers in their area.

Our Vision

To create a world where finding skilled professionals is as easy as a few clicks, regardless of digital literacy or location.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, ProFind started as a small initiative to help local artisans and service providers in Delhi gain more visibility. Today, we've grown into a platform serving multiple cities across India, with plans to expand nationwide. Our team consists of passionate individuals who believe in the power of technology to create economic opportunities for all.
